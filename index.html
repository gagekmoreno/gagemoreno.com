About

I'm a postdoctoral fellow in the Sabeti Lab at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, where I work on genomic epidemiology of respiratory pathogens. My research leverages large-scale pathogen genomic surveillance to understand how viruses like SARS-CoV-2 and RSV spread through communities.

I received my Ph.D. in Cellular and Molecular Pathology from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I characterized SARS-CoV-2 introduction and spread in southern Wisconsin during the early pandemic. My work has been published in Cell, Nature Communications, NEJM, Nature Medicine, and other leading journals.
